April 2026: Stories Behind the Science

❤️ Stories Behind the Science

Dr. Perak recently co-chaired an American Heart Association consensus meeting on women's cardiovascular health. At the meeting, she met women whose experiences with heart disease and stroke are the reason studies like H2H exist. Read their stories:

  • Shanelle Dawes — heart attack at 35
  • Tania Saiz — stroke at 47
  • Lori Sepich — two heart attacks

📰 In the News

A new American Heart Association report projects that nearly 6 in 10 women could have cardiovascular disease by 2050 — with risk factors showing up at younger ages. This is part of why H2H exists: to understand what families need now so we can change these trends.

March 2026: Your Words, Our Why

❤️ ❤️ ❤️

Your Words, Our Why

What H2H families have shared with us

"

I joined because I think research is the only way our society will learn and understand more about healthcare and I'm passionate about women's health being heard and doctors knowing more about it.

"

I hesitated because I feel worried and vulnerable about putting my information out there. I hope this will give H2H the information to support families and educate them on being healthier. I want to be an example for my children and I'm trying to break cycles and normalize better eating and exercise habits.

"

My children are small and my husband works opposite shift to me. We have about 3 hours for homework, dinner and bath time before bedtime. It isn't enough time for me to include a trip to the gym.

"

Finding a way to get regular exercise into my new routines as a mom of 2 was key. Pairing down my recipe box to the easiest stuff for weeknight meals — lower stress.

"

I am joining because I believe that the health of the mother most definitely is neglected when we have multiple young children. I am excited to see the impact this study will make!

These are just a few of the responses we've received — we couldn't share them all here, but every one is read and is shaping H2H. Thank you for your words!

February 2026: Heart Month Moment ❤️ Tiny Pause, Big Love

Thank you for sharing with H2H this month! February is Heart Month—a reminder that caring for your heart also means caring for your whole self.

Parenting is a marathon, not a sprint, and just like your heart, it needs steady moments of rest to keep going strong. Even a few deep breaths can settle your heart and reconnect you to the big love inside it - love you can then share with yourself and your family.

Take just 30 seconds to one minute to pause, breathe, and refocus. Press play and breathe with us—when you're ready, let's begin.
