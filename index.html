Our Mission

We're transforming how families build and maintain heart health across generations. Our research is grounded in the American Heart Association's Life's Essential 8 - the evidence-based framework for cardiovascular health that's linked not just to heart outcomes, but to quality of life, psychological health, and healthy longevity.

🔬 Innovative Research

Studying critical periods across the lifespan when interventions can have the biggest impact on family heart health - from preconception through adolescence and into parenthood.

👥 Participant-Informed

Partnering with diverse families through interviews, advisory boards, and feedback to ensure our programs are relevant, accessible, and effective for those who need them most.

🎯 Evidence-Based

Translating research findings into practical, scalable programs that healthcare providers and families can actually use.

❤️❤️ Heart-to-Heart Study (H2H)

Enrolling by Invitation

Who can participate: Moms of children ages 0-5 in Illinois and their families

Our Research Impact

AMP Lab research directly influences clinical practice and public health policy worldwide

🤰🏻 Maternal-Child Heart Health

Key Finding: Maternal heart health during pregnancy strongly predicts children's heart health in adolescence - even 13+ years later.

Published in JAMA • Selected as AHA Top Scientific Advance of 2021

🎯 Teen Prevention Power

Key Finding: If all teens maintained good heart health, 63% of premature heart disease and 81% of early heart-related deaths could be prevented.

Published in JACC • Top 100 JACC Publications

📊 Life's Essential 8

Key Contribution: Helped create the updated American Heart Association framework now used worldwide to measure and improve cardiovascular health.

Published in Circulation • Foundation for AMP Lab studies

🏥 Clinical Practice Change

Real-World Impact: AMP Lab research has been cited by 37+ clinical guidelines and professional statements, directly changing how doctors care for families.

Guidelines from AHA, ACC, CDC • International influence
