What Makes H2H Groundbreaking: Your Role in the Discovery

We're solving critical gaps in family health research - and you're the key to each breakthrough

Research Gap #1: How do we protect busy moms' heart health?

A New Strategy: H2H reaches moms through their young kids - then listens to what moms say they need.

Moms with young children often lose ground on their own heart health. They may skip their own doctor visits but never miss their kids' appointments. H2H meets moms where they are to find out what help they actually need and want. This creates programs that work for real families.

H2H moms are solving the Gap #1 puzzle of what really works for heart health during early parenting

(All 5,000 participants)

You're helping us understand what works for busy moms during the demanding early parenting years. Your participation shows us how to protect mom's heart health when life is at its busiest.

Your impact: Future moms won't have to choose between their health and their family's needs.

Research Gap #2: Why can't we break the cycle of heart health problems?

The Missing Piece: Only by following H2H moms from before pregnancy through early childhood can we capture the real story.

Scientists have long puzzled over WHEN heart health matters most. H2H uniquely captures the complete journey - before, during, and after pregnancy - including the 50% of pregnancies that aren't planned. Finally, we can learn what happens in real families.

H2H moms are unlocking Gap #2's critical piece of the puzzle

(~650 pregnancies expected)

If you become pregnant, you help us understand the complete journey. You're filling in a unique part of the roadmap - showing us exactly when heart health matters most for breaking the cycle.

Your impact: Your pregnancy journey helps solve a puzzle that impacts every future generation.

Research Gap #3: But will it actually work?

Real Families, Real Voices: In H2H, parents - moms, dads, and co-parents from all kinds of families - are the real experts.

Health programs often fail because they're designed without input from the people who need them. H2H directly asks diverse families what really affects their health and what help would actually work.

H2H families are closing Gap #3 to make programs that work

(All 5,000 participants)

H2H surveys ask what REALLY helps or gets in the way of your healthy habits - from money to stress to support. Some parents also share deeper thoughts in video chats about what would really help. Your answers help us make programs that fit real life.

Your impact: Your voice ensures future programs fit real families' lives.

The Next Breakthrough

Prior research showed pregnancy heart health matters. But now it's time for the next step. H2H unlocks the complete journey - before, during, and after pregnancy - including the whole family. Together, we're creating the roadmap for healthier families.

What H2H Moms Do

When You Join:

📋

Welcome Survey

30 minutes

  • • Health history
  • • Current habits
  • • Health habit drivers (motivation, time, money, etc.)
  • • Stress levels
  • • Sources of stress (parenting, work, etc.)
  • • Family situation
🏥

Medical Records

Give permission

  • • You give consent
  • • We handle paperwork
  • • Tell us where you receive care
  • • We collect specific health info like:
  • - Blood pressure & blood sugar
  • - Medical diagnoses (diabetes, etc.)
  • - Labor & delivery records
  • • NOT your entire medical record
📱

Monthly Check-ins

5 minutes

  • • Quick health updates
  • • Life changes
  • • Stay connected

If You Become Pregnant:

📋

Additional Surveys (up to 4)

30 minutes each

  • • Over 2-3 years
  • • Track key moments
  • • Pregnancy
  • • Baby's arrival & milestones

Your choice

Baby's Records
