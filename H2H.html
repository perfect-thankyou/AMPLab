What Makes H2H Groundbreaking: Your Role in the Discovery

We're solving critical gaps in family health research - and you're the key to each breakthrough

Research Gap #1: How do we protect busy moms' heart health?

A New Strategy: H2H reaches moms through their young kids - then listens to what moms say they need.

Moms with young children often lose ground on their own heart health. They may skip their own doctor visits but never miss their kids' appointments. H2H meets moms where they are to find out what help they actually need and want. This creates programs that work for real families.

H2H moms are solving the Gap #1 puzzle of what really works for heart health during early parenting

(All 5,000 participants)

You're helping us understand what works for busy moms during the demanding early parenting years. Your participation shows us how to protect mom's heart health when life is at its busiest.

Your impact: Future moms won't have to choose between their health and their family's needs.

Research Gap #2: Why can't we break the cycle of heart health problems?

The Missing Piece: Only by following H2H moms from before pregnancy through early childhood can we capture the real story.

Scientists have long puzzled over WHEN heart health matters most. H2H uniquely captures the complete journey - before, during, and after pregnancy - including the 50% of pregnancies that aren't planned. Finally, we can learn what happens in real families.

H2H moms are unlocking Gap #2's critical piece of the puzzle

(~650 pregnancies expected)

If you become pregnant, you help us understand the complete journey. You're filling in a unique part of the roadmap - showing us exactly when heart health matters most for breaking the cycle.

Your impact: Your pregnancy journey helps solve a puzzle that impacts every future generation.

Research Gap #3: But will it actually work?

Real Families, Real Voices: In H2H, parents - moms, dads, and co-parents from all kinds of families - are the real experts.

Health programs often fail because they're designed without input from the people who need them. H2H directly asks diverse families what really affects their health and what help would actually work.

H2H families are closing Gap #3 to make programs that work

(All 5,000 participants)

H2H surveys ask what REALLY helps or gets in the way of your healthy habits - from money to stress to support. Some parents also share deeper thoughts in video chats about what would really help. Your answers help us make programs that fit real life.

Your impact: Your voice ensures future programs fit real families' lives.

The Next Breakthrough

Prior research showed pregnancy heart health matters. But now it's time for the next step. H2H unlocks the complete journey - before, during, and after pregnancy - including the whole family. Together, we're creating the roadmap for healthier families.

Your Privacy & Rights

Multiple layers of protection for your information - click each card to learn more

🏥 Independent Oversight

Reviewed by multiple institutions

Reviewed by Lurie Children's Hospital and partner institutions. People outside the research team ensure we're protecting participants and conducting ethical research.

🛡️ Legal Protections

NIH Certificate of Confidentiality

This certificate provides federal legal protection for your information. Researchers generally cannot be forced to share information that could identify you, even by courts.
Learn more about Certificates of Confidentiality →

🔒 Data Security

Multiple security safeguards

Your information is stored in HIPAA-compliant secure systems with strict access controls. Survey answers are kept completely separate from contact information. All data transmission is encrypted, and only authorized research team members can access study data. Our team consulted legal and security experts to design the strongest protections possible.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Privacy

Your answers stay private

If you invite family members to join H2H, we never share your answers with them, and we don't share their answers with you. Everyone's information stays completely private.

✅ Your Control

You're always in charge

You can:
  • Stop participating at any time
  • Skip any questions you don't want to answer
  • Choose whether to invite others
  • Ask questions anytime

⚠️ Important Note

H2H is research, not medical care

Remember: H2H is research to help future families, not medical care for current health issues. Always contact your healthcare provider for medical questions or concerns about your health. Your participation won't affect your medical care.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join H2H?
H2H enrollment is by invitation only through healthcare systems or research partners. If you received an invitation, use your personal link to join. We're continuing to expand to new locations.
Is this only for moms planning another pregnancy?
Not at all! Every participant matters equally. We're studying heart health for ALL moms of young children, regardless of future pregnancy plans. Your participation helps create better health programs for all families.
What if I don't want to include my partner or my child's father?
That's completely your choice. We never contact anyone without your permission. Your participation won't be affected in any way if you choose not to invite others.
If I invite family members, do you share our answers?
No! We don't share your study answers with anyone you invite, and we don't share their answers with you or with each other. Everyone's information stays private.
How is my information protected?
We have multiple layers of protection: NIH Certificate of Confidentiality, institutional oversight, secure data storage, and your information kept separate from your identity. Your name never appears in any reports. See the Privacy section above for complete details.
What if I need health help during the study?
H2H is research, not medical care. You should always talk to your own doctor about any health concerns. We provide resources and reminders to work with your healthcare providers, and we can help connect you with resources if needed.
Can I quit if I change my mind?
Of course! You can stop participating at any time without affecting any medical care you receive. Just contact us and we'll remove you from future surveys.
Will I get my personal results?
We don't provide individual medical results - that's something to discuss with your healthcare provider. But we will send you exciting updates about what H2H is learning and how it's making a real difference for families!
What if I move during the study?
No problem! Since H2H is completely online, you can participate from anywhere. Just update your contact information in your monthly survey.
What technology do I need?
You can use any device with internet access - smartphone, tablet, or computer. If you need help with technology or internet access, contact us and we can help you find local resources.
What if I don't have reliable internet?
Contact us! We can help you find local resources like libraries with free internet, or discuss other options for participation.
How long do surveys take?
Initial survey: 30 minutes. Monthly surveys: 5 minutes. If you become pregnant: 1-4 additional 30-minute surveys over 2-3 years.
